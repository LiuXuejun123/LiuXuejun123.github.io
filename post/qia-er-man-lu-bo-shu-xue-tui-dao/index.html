Kalman fiter 数学推导过程

贝叶斯

(1)bel⁡‾(xt)=∫p(xt∣ut,xt−1) bel (xt−1)dxt−1\overline{\operatorname{bel}}\left(x_{t}\right)=\int p\left(x_{t} \mid u_{t}, x_{t-1}\right) \text { bel }\left(x_{t-1}\right) d x_{t-1}\tag{1} \\ bel(xt​)=∫p(xt​∣ut​,xt−1​) bel (xt−1​)dxt−1​(1)

(2) bel (xt)=ηp(zt∣xt)bel⁡‾(xt)\begin{array}{l} \text { bel }\left(x_{t}\right)=\eta p\left(z_{t} \mid x_{t}\right) \overline{\operatorname{bel}}\left(x_{t}\right)\tag{2} \end{array}  bel (xt​)=ηp(zt​∣xt​)bel(xt​)​(2)

状态方程：

(3)xt=Atxt−1+Btut+εt\boldsymbol{x}_{t}=\boldsymbol{A}_{t} \boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}+\boldsymbol{B}_{t} \boldsymbol{u}_{t}+\boldsymbol{\varepsilon}_{t}\tag{3} xt​=At​xt−1​+Bt​ut​+εt​(3)

观测方程

(4)zt=Ctxt+δt\boldsymbol{z}_{t}=\boldsymbol{C}_{t} \boldsymbol{x}_{t}+\delta_{t}\tag{4} zt​=Ct​xt​+δt​(4)

后验状态的均值由Atxt−1+Btut\boldsymbol{A}_{t}\boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}+\boldsymbol{B}_{t}\boldsymbol{u}_{t}At​xt−1​+Bt​ut​ 给定，方差由Rt\boldsymbol{R}_{t}Rt​ 给定

(5)p(xt∣ut,xt−1)=det⁡(2πRt)−12exp⁡{−12(xt−Atxt−1−Btut)TRt−1(xt−Atxt−1−Btut)}p\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t} \mid u_{t}, \boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}\right)=\operatorname{det}\left(2 \pi R_{t}\right)^{-\frac{1}{2}} \exp \left\{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}-A_{t} \boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}-B_{t} u_{t}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} R_{t}^{-1}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}-A_{t} \boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}-B_{t} u_{t}\right)\right\}\tag{5} p(xt​∣ut​,xt−1​)=det(2πRt​)−21​exp{−21​(xt​−At​xt−1​−Bt​ut​)TRt−1​(xt​−At​xt−1​−Bt​ut​)}(5)

测量概率由下面的多元正态分布给定

(6)p(zt∣xt)=det⁡(2πQt)−12exp⁡{−12(zt−Ctxt)TQt−1(zt−Ctxt)}p\left(\boldsymbol{z}_{t} \mid {x}_{t}\right)=\operatorname{det}\left(2 \pi Q_{t}\right)^{-\frac{1}{2}} \exp \left\{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\boldsymbol{z}_{t}-C_{t} \boldsymbol{x}_{t}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1}\left(\boldsymbol{z}_{t}-C_{t} \boldsymbol{x}_{t}\right)\right\}\tag{6} p(zt​∣xt​)=det(2πQt​)−21​exp{−21​(zt​−Ct​xt​)TQt−1​(zt​−Ct​xt​)}(6)

预测:

(7)bel(xt−1)=exp⁡{−12(xt−1−μt−1)TΣt−1−1(xt−1−μt−1)}bel( \boldsymbol{x}_{t-1})=\exp \left\{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}-\mu_{t-1}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} \mathrm{\Sigma}_{t-1}^{-1}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}-\mu_{t-1}\right)\right\}\tag{7} bel(xt−1​)=exp{−21​(xt−1​−μt−1​)TΣt−1−1​(xt−1​−μt−1​)}(7)

将式（1）写成高斯指数形式

(8)bel⁡‾(xt)=η∫exp⁡{−12(xt−Atxt−1−Btut)TRt−1(xt−Atxt−1−Btut)}exp⁡{−12(xt−1−μt−1)TΣt−1−1(xt−1−μt−1)}dxt−1\begin{aligned} \overline{\operatorname{bel}}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}\right)=& \eta \int \exp \left\{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}-\boldsymbol{A}_{t} \boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}-\boldsymbol{B}_{t} u_{t}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} R_{t}^{-1}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}-\boldsymbol{A}_{t} \boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}-\boldsymbol{B}_{t} u_{t}\right)\right\} \\ & \exp \left\{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}-\mu_{t-1}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} \Sigma_{t-1}^{-1}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}-\mu_{t-1}\right)\right\} \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x}_{t-1} \end{aligned}\tag{8} bel(xt​)=​η∫exp{−21​(xt​−At​xt−1​−Bt​ut​)TRt−1​(xt​−At​xt−1​−Bt​ut​)}exp{−21​(xt−1​−μt−1​)TΣt−1−1​(xt−1​−μt−1​)}dxt−1​​(8)

简写可以写成

(9)bel⁡‾(xt)=η∫exp⁡{−Lt}dxt−1\overline{\operatorname{bel}}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}\right)=\eta \int \exp \left\{-L_{t}\right\} \mathrm{d}{\boldsymbol{x}}_{t-1}\tag{9} bel(xt​)=η∫exp{−Lt​}dxt−1​(9)

(10)Lt=12(xt−Atxt−1−Btut)TRt−1(xt−Atxt−1−Btut)+12(xt−1−μt−1)TΣt−1−1(xt−1−μt−1)\begin{aligned} L_{t}=& \frac{1}{2}\left(x_{t}-A_{t} x_{t-1}-B_{t} u_{t}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} R_{t}^{-1}\left(x_{t}-A_{t} x_{t-1}-B_{t} u_{t}\right)+\\ & \frac{1}{2}\left(x_{t-1}-\mu_{t-1}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} \Sigma_{t-1}^{-1}\left(x_{t-1}-\mu_{t-1}\right) \end{aligned}\tag{10} Lt​=​21​(xt​−At​xt−1​−Bt​ut​)TRt−1​(xt​−At​xt−1​−Bt​ut​)+21​(xt−1​−μt−1​)TΣt−1−1​(xt−1​−μt−1​)​(10)

注意贝叶斯公式中的积分对象：

我们将上式分成两部分Lt=Lt(xt)+Lt(xt，xt−1)L_{t}=L_{t}(x_{t})+L_{t}(x_{t}，x_{t-1})Lt​=Lt​(xt​)+Lt​(xt​，xt−1​)

(11)Lt(xt)=+12(xt−Btut)TRt−1(xt−Btut)+12μt−1T∑t−1−1μt−1−12[AtTRt−1(xt−Btut)+∑t−1−1μt−1]T(AtTRt−1At+Σt−1−1)−1[AtTRt−1(xt−Btut)+Σt−1−1μt−1]\begin{array}{l} L_{t}\left(x_{t}\right)=+\frac{1}{2}\left(x_{t}-B_{t} u_{t}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} R_{t}^{-1}\left(x_{t}-B_{t} u_{t}\right)+\frac{1}{2} \mu_{t-1}^{\mathrm{T}} \sum_{t-1}^{-1} \mu_{t-1}- \\ \frac{1}{2}\left[A_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} R_{t}^{-1}\left(x_{t}-B_{t} u_{t}\right)+\sum_{t-1}^{-1} \mu_{t-1}\right]^{\mathrm{T}}\left(A_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} R_{t}^{-1} A_{t}+\Sigma_{t-1}^{-1}\right)^{-1}\left[A_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} R_{t}^{-1}\left(x_{t}-B_{t} u_{t}\right)+\Sigma_{t-1}^{-1} \mu_{t-1}\right] \end{array}\tag{11} Lt​(xt​)=+21​(xt​−Bt​ut​)TRt−1​(xt​−Bt​ut​)+21​μt−1T​∑t−1−1​μt−1​−21​[AtT​Rt−1​(xt​−Bt​ut​)+∑t−1−1​μt−1​]T(AtT​Rt−1​At​+Σt−1−1​)−1[AtT​Rt−1​(xt​−Bt​ut​)+Σt−1−1​μt−1​]​(11)

那么可得：

(12)bel⁡‾(xt)=ηexp⁡{−Lt(xt)}∫exp⁡{−Lt(xt−1,xt)}dxt−1=ηexp⁡{−Lt(xt)}\begin{aligned} \overline{\operatorname{bel}}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}\right) &=\eta \exp \left\{-L_{t}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}\right)\right\} \int \exp \left\{-L_{t}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t-1}, \boldsymbol{x}_{t}\right)\right\} \mathrm{d} \boldsymbol{x}_{t-1} \\ &=\eta \exp \left\{-L_{t}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}\right)\right\} \end{aligned}\tag{12} bel(xt​)​=ηexp{−Lt​(xt​)}∫exp{−Lt​(xt−1​,xt​)}dxt−1​=ηexp{−Lt​(xt​)}​(12)

其表明了 bel‾(xt)\overline{bel}(x_{t})bel(xt​) 确实服从高斯分布那么其均值和方差为Lt(xt)L_{t}(x_{t})Lt​(xt​) 的极小值和曲率。

通过计算一阶导和二阶导可以轻易获得：

(13)∂Lt(xt)∂xt=Rt−1(xt−Btut)−Rt−1At(AtTRt−1At+Σt−1−1)−1[AtTRt−1(xt−Btut)+Σt−1−1μt−1]=[Rt−1−Rt−1At(AtTRt−1At+Σt−1−1)−1AtTRt−1](xt−Btut)−Rt−1At(AtTRt−1At+Σt−1−1)−1Σt−1−1μt−1\begin{aligned} \frac{\partial L_{t}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}\right)}{\partial \boldsymbol{x}_{t}}=& \boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}-\boldsymbol{B}_{t} \boldsymbol{u}_{t}\right)-\boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1} \boldsymbol{A}_{t}\left(\boldsymbol{A}_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1} \boldsymbol{A}_{t}+\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{t-1}^{-1}\right)^{-1}\left[\boldsymbol{A}_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1}\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}-\boldsymbol{B}_{t} \boldsymbol{u}_{t}\right)+\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{t-1}^{-1} \boldsymbol{\mu}_{t-1}\right] \\ =&\left[\boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1}-\boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1} \boldsymbol{A}_{t}\left(\boldsymbol{A}_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1} \boldsymbol{A}_{t}+\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{t-1}^{-1}\right)^{-1} \boldsymbol{A}_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1}\right]\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}-\boldsymbol{B}_{t} u_{t}\right)-\\ & \boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1} \boldsymbol{A}_{t}\left(\boldsymbol{A}_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol{R}_{t}^{-1} \boldsymbol{A}_{t}+\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{t-1}^{-1}\right)^{-1} \boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{t-1}^{-1} \boldsymbol{\mu}_{t-1} \end{aligned}\tag{13} ∂xt​∂Lt​(xt​)​==​Rt−1​(xt​−Bt​ut​)−Rt−1​At​(AtT​Rt−1​At​+Σt−1−1​)−1[AtT​Rt−1​(xt​−Bt​ut​)+Σt−1−1​μt−1​][Rt−1​−Rt−1​At​(AtT​Rt−1​At​+Σt−1−1​)−1AtT​Rt−1​](xt​−Bt​ut​)−Rt−1​At​(AtT​Rt−1​At​+Σt−1−1​)−1Σt−1−1​μt−1​​(13)

使用求逆引理(inversion lemma)

(14)Rt−1−Rt−1At(At⊤Rt−1At+Σt−1−1)−1AtTRt−1=(Rt+AtΣt−1AtT)−1R_{t}^{-1}-R_{t}^{-1} A_{t}\left(A_{t}^{\top} R_{t}^{-1} A_{t}+\Sigma_{t-1}^{-1}\right)^{-1} A_{t}^{T} R_{t}^{-1}=\left(R_{t}+A_{t} \Sigma_{t-1} A_{t}^{T}\right)^{-1}\tag{14} Rt−1​−Rt−1​At​(At⊤​Rt−1​At​+Σt−1−1​)−1AtT​Rt−1​=(Rt​+At​Σt−1​AtT​)−1(14)

取一阶导为0可以得到：

(15)xt=Btut+Atμt−1\boldsymbol{x}_{t}=\boldsymbol{B}_{t}u_{t}+A_{t}\mu_{t-1}\tag{15} xt​=Bt​ut​+At​μt−1​(15)

二阶导可以得到：

(16)∂2Lt(xt)∂xt2=(AtΣt−1−1AtT+Rt)−1\frac{\partial^{2} L_{t}\left(x_{t}\right)}{\partial x_{t}^{2}}=\left(A_{t} \Sigma_{t-1}^{-1} A_{t}^{\mathrm{T}}+R_{t}\right)^{-1}\tag{16} ∂xt2​∂2Lt​(xt​)​=(At​Σt−1−1​AtT​+Rt​)−1(16)

其逆矩阵就是bel‾(xt)\overline{bel}(\boldsymbol{x}_{t})bel(xt​)的协方差。

如上可以获得卡尔曼滤波的预测部分

(17)μˉt=Atμt−1+BtutΣˉt=AtΣt−1AtT+Rt\begin{array}{l} \bar{\mu}_{t}=A_{t} \mu_{t-1}+B_{t} u_{t} \\ \bar{\Sigma}_{t}=A_{t} \Sigma_{t-1} A_{t}^{T}+R_{t} \end{array}\tag{17} μˉ​t​=At​μt−1​+Bt​ut​Σˉt​=At​Σt−1​AtT​+Rt​​(17)

更新

我们将预测bel‾(xt)\overline{bel}(x_{t})bel(xt​)的均值和方差一起写成高斯形式 bel‾(xt)∼N(μ‾t,Σ‾t)\overline{bel}(x_{t})\sim \mathcal{N}(\overline{\mu}_{t},\overline{\Sigma}_{t})bel(xt​)∼N(μ​t​,Σt​)

(18)bel⁡(xt)=ηp(zt∣xt)bel⁡‾(xt)\operatorname{bel}\left(x_{t}\right)=\eta p\left(z_{t} \mid x_{t}\right) \overline{\operatorname{bel}}\left(x_{t}\right)\tag{18} bel(xt​)=ηp(zt​∣xt​)bel(xt​)(18)

p(zt∣xt)∼N(Ctxt,Qt)p(z_{t}\mid x_{t})\sim\mathcal{N}(\boldsymbol{C_{t}x_{t}},Q_{t})p(zt​∣xt​)∼N(Ct​xt​,Qt​)

那么将更新写成高斯形式，并且如同上式取指数部分。

(19)Jt=12(zt−Ctxt)TQt−1(zt−Ctxt)+12(xt−μˉt)TΣˉt−1(xt−μˉt)J_{t}=\frac{1}{2}\left(z_{t}-C_{t} x_{t}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1}\left(z_{t}-C_{t} x_{t}\right)+\frac{1}{2}\left(x_{t}-\bar{\mu}_{t}\right)^{\mathrm{T}} \bar{\Sigma}_{t}^{-1}\left(x_{t}-\bar{\mu}_{t}\right)\tag{19} Jt​=21​(zt​−Ct​xt​)TQt−1​(zt​−Ct​xt​)+21​(xt​−μˉ​t​)TΣˉt−1​(xt​−μˉ​t​)(19)

分别对其求一阶导二阶导，极值点为后验，也就是更新后的均值，二阶导为更新后协方差的逆。

(20)∂J∂xt=−CtTQt−1(zt−Ctxt)+Σˉt−1(xt−μˉt)∂2J∂xt2=CtTQt−1Ct+Σˉt−1\begin{array}{c} \frac{\partial J}{\partial x_{t}}=-C_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1}\left(z_{t}-C_{t} x_{t}\right)+\bar{\Sigma}_{t}^{-1}\left(x_{t}-\bar{\mu}_{t}\right) \\ \frac{\partial^{2} J}{\partial x_{t}^{2}}=C_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1} C_{t}+\bar{\Sigma}_{t}^{-1} \end{array}\tag{20} ∂xt​∂J​=−CtT​Qt−1​(zt​−Ct​xt​)+Σˉt−1​(xt​−μˉ​t​)∂xt2​∂2J​=CtT​Qt−1​Ct​+Σˉt−1​​(20)

那么我们可以获得后验bel(xt)bel(x_{t})bel(xt​) 的协方差：

(21)Σt=(Ct⊤Qt−1Ct+Σ‾t−1)−1\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{t}=\left(\boldsymbol{C}_{t}^{\top} \boldsymbol{Q}_{t}^{-1} \boldsymbol{C}_{t}+\overline{\boldsymbol{\Sigma}}_{t}^{-1}\right)^{-1}\tag{21} Σt​=(Ct⊤​Qt−1​Ct​+Σt−1​)−1(21)

取一阶导等于0

(22)CtTQt−1(zt−Ctμt)=Σˉt−1(μt−μˉt)C_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1}\left(z_{t}-C_{t} \mu_{t}\right)=\bar{\Sigma}_{t}^{-1}\left(\mu_{t}-\bar{\mu}_{t}\right)\tag{22} CtT​Qt−1​(zt​−Ct​μt​)=Σˉt−1​(μt​−μˉ​t​)(22)

对等式左边做点处理，括号内加减一个Ctμ‾tC_{t}\overline{\mu}_{t}Ct​μ​t​

(23)CtTQt−1(zt−Ctμt+Ctμ‾t−Ctμ‾t)分成两部分：CtTQt−1(zt−Ctμ‾t)−CtTQt−1(Ctμt−Ctμ‾t)C_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1}\left(z_{t}-C_{t} \mu_{t}+C_{t}\overline{\mu}_{t}-C_{t}\overline{\mu}_{t}\right)\\分成两部分：C_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1}\left(z_{t}-C_{t} \overline{\mu}_{t}\right)-C_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1}\left(C_{t}\mu_{t}-C_{t} \overline{\mu}_{t}\right)\tag{23} CtT​Qt−1​(zt​−Ct​μt​+Ct​μ​t​−Ct​μ​t​)分成两部分：CtT​Qt−1​(zt​−Ct​μ​t​)−CtT​Qt−1​(Ct​μt​−Ct​μ​t​)(23)

带入到（22）可以得到：

(24)CtTQt−1(zt−Ctμˉt)=(CtTQt−1Ct+Σˉt−1)⎵=Σt−1(μt−μˉt)C_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1}\left(z_{t}-C_{t} \bar{\mu}_{t}\right)=\underbrace{\left(C_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1} C_{t}+\bar{\Sigma}_{t}^{-1}\right)}_{=\Sigma_{t}^{-1}}\left(\mu_{t}-\bar{\mu}_{t}\right)\tag{24} CtT​Qt−1​(zt​−Ct​μˉ​t​)==Σt−1​(CtT​Qt−1​Ct​+Σˉt−1​)​​(μt​−μˉ​t​)(24)

(25)ΣtCtTQt−1(zt−Ctμˉt)=μt−μˉt\Sigma_{t} C_{t}^{\mathrm{T}} Q_{t}^{-1}(z_{t}-C_{t}\bar{\mu}_{t})=\mu_{t}-\bar{\mu}_{t}\tag{25} Σt​CtT​Qt−1​(zt​−Ct​μˉ​t​)=μt​−μˉ​t​(25)

取卡尔曼增益KtK_{t}Kt​
